HOW TO PLAY

Duo Clash is a real-time strategic fighting game. Timing is everything.

GAMEPLAY:

1. Both players submit moves in a 1-second timeframe.

2. Moves are resolved simultaneously, HP and energy are updated.

ENERGY COST:

  • L/R Slash < O.head = Stab < Block.
  • Energy recovers slowly each turn.
  • If Energy hits 0, you cannot move for 1 second.

DAMAGE:

Health deteroriates when hit. Block prevents damage.

COUNTERS (0 COST / 0 DAMAGE):

  • Overhead vs Overhead
  • Stab vs Stab
  • Opposite Slashes (Left vs Right)

Victory goes to the player whose opponent is reduced to zero Health.
